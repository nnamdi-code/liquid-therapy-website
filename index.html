Frequent Questions

Everything you
need to know

Do you supply the drinks?
We supply select drinks depending on the service chosen. For Welcome Service, house wine is supplied in certain tiers. For Drink Care and Bar Setup, drinks are supplied by the host. Final selections are confirmed during the planning phase.
How many drinks should I expect per guest?
Drink quantities are estimated using rounds, based on a standard service window. Welcome Service typically assumes 2 to 3 rounds depending on the tier. Extended service requires additional staffing or scope.
What does 'rounds assumed' mean?
A round refers to one full-service pass across guests during the designated window. Rounds are used for planning and staffing estimates, not strict counting.
Do Immersive Sessions include drinks?
No. Immersive Sessions focus on facilitated experiences and guided interactions. Drinks and bar services are added separately.
What is the difference between Welcome Service, Drink Care and Bar Setup?
Welcome Service focuses on arrival moments and setting the tone. Drink Care oversees drinks supplied by the host. Bar Setup provides the physical bar structure. Services may be booked independently or combined.
Do you offer tasting sessions or cocktail workshops?
Yes. We host tasting sessions and guided sensory workshops that explore therapy-inspired mixology, scent pairings, and playful cocktail experiences based on availability.
Is there a minimum service commitment?
Yes. Each service has a minimum commitment based on scope, staffing and preparation. Details are outlined in our separate rate card.
Which areas do you serve?
We are proudly Lagos-based and serve across the state. For out-of-state service, we accommodate requests based on availability. International events are quoted on request to cover flights, accommodation, freight and visas.
Do you offer customisation or add-ons?
Yes. Styling enhancements, keepsakes, extended service and additional elements are available. See our Service Extensions section for the full list.
How far in advance should I book?
For intimate gatherings, we recommend at least two weeks' notice. For larger events and corporate experiences, four to six weeks ensures optimal customisation.
